**5.4. REPORTS AND DASHBOARDS (EXECUTIVE DASHBOARD, SALES DASHBOARD, CS DASHBOARD)**

In the target architecture, reporting and visualization of key metrics are implemented
at two levels:
- **Tableau** – the primary tool for Executive Dashboard, Sales Dashboard,
  and CS Dashboard, with live connections to Snowflake and Salesforce.
- **Salesforce Dashboards** – simplified versions for quick access within the CRM
  (using standard reports and dashboards).

All dashboards are built on data that updates in real time (via reverse ETL and Platform Events),
enabling leadership and teams to make decisions based on current information.
Each dashboard is tailored to a specific audience: C‑level, sales management,
and customer success managers.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.4.1. EXECUTIVE DASHBOARD (FOR CRO, CPO, INVESTORS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**Objective:** Provide senior leadership with an instant snapshot of business status,
trends, and critical risks. The dashboard should be concise but contain all key metrics
defined in Section 2.3.

**Data sources:** Salesforce (Account, Opportunity, Closed Won), Snowflake
(aggregated metrics, forecasts).

**Refresh:** real‑time (live connection in Tableau); extracts update every 15 minutes.

**Dashboard structure (tableau)**

| Block                     | Content                                                                 | Visualization               |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| **ARR and Growth**        | Current ARR ($M), YoY growth (%), QoQ growth (%), quarter‑end forecast  | Large numbers, spline chart |
| **Net Retention (NRR)**   | NRR in %, trend over last 4 quarters, breakdown by segment (Enterprise, Mid‑Market, SMB) | Line chart, bars            |
| **Pipeline Coverage**     | Ratio of open pipeline value to quarterly quota (target >3×). Color coding: green (>3), yellow (2–3), red (<2) | Indicator, column with threshold |
| **Forecast Accuracy**     | MAPE for current and previous quarter, comparison of forecast vs. actual by month | Line chart, bars            |
| **Win Rate (Enterprise)** | Win rate by segment, region, stage. Trend over last 4 quarters          | Table, sparklines           |
| **Health Score**          | Customer distribution by zones (green/yellow/red) in % and absolute numbers. Trend over last 3 months | Donut chart, bars           |
| **Top 10 Opportunities**  | List of the 10 largest deals in pipeline with close probability, amount, expected close date, responsible AE | Sortable table              |
| **Alerts**                | Critical alerts: Pipeline Coverage <2×, Forecast Accuracy <80%, Churn Spike >10%, Health Score red zone >20% | Panel with color coding     |

**Metric details**

*ARR and Growth*
- Formula: sum of annual contract values of all active customers.
- Source: `Amount` field on Opportunity with status `Closed Won` and active `Contract_Status__c`.
- Forecast: based on weighted pipeline (see Section 6.4).

*Net Retention (NRR)*
- Formula: `(ARR_start + Expansion – Churn) / ARR_start`
- Calculated monthly by financial agents, result stored in Snowflake and displayed in the dashboard.

*Forecast Accuracy*
- MAPE = `(1/n) * Σ |(actual – forecast) / actual|`
- Forecast taken from the model (Win_Probability), actual from closed deals.

*Health Score*
- Green zone: H ≥ 0.8; yellow: 0.5 ≤ H < 0.8; red: H < 0.5.
- Source: `Health_Score__c` field on Account (updated by agents).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.4.2. SALES DASHBOARD (FOR VP SALES, AE, SALES STRATEGIST)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**Objective:** Provide sales leadership and individual contributors with a transparent view of
plan attainment, activity, pipeline quality, and problem areas.

**Data sources:** Salesforce (Opportunity, Account, Task), Snowflake
(aggregated metrics by AE).

**Refresh:** real‑time (live connection) for metrics dependent on deal status;
historical data every 15 minutes.

**Dashboard structure**

| Block                         | Content                                                                 | Visualization               |
|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Quota and attainment (YTD)** | For each AE: quota, actual closed revenue, attainment %. Sorted descending. | Table, bars                 |
| **Pipeline by AE**            | For each AE: total pipeline (Active Opportunities), coverage ratio (Pipeline / Quota), quarter‑end forecast | Table, indicators           |
| **Activity**                  | Number of meetings, demos, emails sent per week; comparison with norm (target for AE). | Sparklines, bars            |
| **MEDDPICC completeness**     | Percentage of deals with filled MEDDPICC fields (MEDDPICC_Completeness__c >0.7) by AE. Impact on win rate. | Table, scatter plot         |
| **Intent signals (for Strategist)** | List of accounts with Account Score >0.7, sorted descending. Fields: ICP_Fit, Intent_Strength, Last_Activity_Date. | Filterable table            |
| **Deal cycle**                | Average deal duration by stage (days). Identify stalled deals (Days_In_Stage > stage average). | Bars, list of stalled       |
| **Won / Lost**                | Loss analysis: reasons (competitors, price, timing) based on comments from MEDDPICC_Fields__c. | Word cloud, table           |

**Metric details**

*Quota and attainment*
- Quota loaded from planning system (Excel/API) and stored in custom object `Sales_Quota__c`.
- Attainment calculated as sum of Amount of closed deals (Closed Won) for the period.

*Pipeline by AE*
- Active deals: Opportunity with Stage not Closed Won/Lost.
- Quarter‑end forecast: sum of (Amount × Win_Probability) for deals with expected close date in current quarter.

*Activity*
- Activity norm set by manager (e.g., 10 meetings per week).
- Data from Task/Event objects where `Type` = `Meeting`, `Demo`, `Call`.

*MEDDPICC completeness*
- Automatically computed from JSON in `MEDDPICC_Fields__c` (analytic agents).
- Impact on win rate: compare win rates for deals with filled vs. unfilled fields.

5.4.3. CS DASHBOARD (FOR VP CS, CSM, AM)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**Objective:** Provide customer success managers with current information on
customer health, churn risk, expansion opportunities, and playbook effectiveness.

**Data sources:** Salesforce (Account, Task, Activity), Gainsight
(health score history, playbook execution), Snowflake (aggregated metrics by CSM).

**Refresh:** real‑time (live connection) for Health Score and Expansion Signals;
historical data every 15 minutes.

**Dashboard structure**

| Block                           | Content                                                                 | Visualization               |
|---------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Health Score (red zone)**     | List of customers with Health_Score__c < 0.5, sorted by priority (lower score = higher priority). Fields: Account Name, Health_Score, Last_Activity_Date, Risk_Flag. | Table, color coding         |
| **Health Score (yellow zone)**  | List of customers with 0.5 ≤ Health_Score__c < 0.8. Fields: Account Name, Health_Score, Last_Activity_Date. | Table                       |
| **Expansion Signals**           | List of customers with Expansion_Signal__c = true, sorted by expansion likelihood (Score). Fields: Account Name, Usage, New_Users_Growth, Feature_Request. | Table                       |
| **Churn Risk**                  | High‑risk customers (Health_Score <0.5 or activity drop >30% in a month) without open tasks for CSM. Button “Create task”. | Table with action           |
| **Onboarding (Time to First Value)** | Average time from deal close to first active use (TTFV) by month. Breakdown by CSM. | Line chart, bars            |
| **Playbook effectiveness**      | Number of playbooks launched (retention, expansion), success rate (led to Health Score improvement or upsell close). | Bars, pie chart             |
| **CSM activity**                | Number of customer meetings, tasks created, playbooks executed per week. Comparison with norm. | Table, sparklines           |
| **NPS and feedback**            | Average NPS by segment, latest comments from surveys. | Chart, word cloud           |

**Metric details**

*Health Score (red/yellow zone)*
- Color zones correspond to thresholds defined in Section 6.2.
- For customers in red zone, a CSM task is automatically created (retention playbook).
- Dashboard allows drilling into customer card to view Health Score history.

*Expansion Signals*
- Trigger conditions: Usage >0.7 and user growth >30% over 30 days, or feature request.
- Source: `Expansion_Signal__c` field on Account (updated by analytic agents).

*Onboarding (TTFV)*
- Definition: date of first product login after deal close minus close date.
- Analytic agents compute TTFV and store in `Opportunity.Onboarding_Time__c` (custom field).

*Playbook effectiveness*
- Playbook success: if Health Score improves within 30 days after a retention playbook, it is considered successful.
- For expansion playbook – if an upsell deal closes.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.4.4. IMPLEMENTATION IN TABLEAU AND SALESFORCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**5.4.4.1. Tableau**

- **Connection:** Live connection to Snowflake (for large volumes) and Salesforce
  (for operational data). Extracts are used for historical dashboards (refresh every 15 minutes).
- **Access rights:** Row‑level security based on user profile:
  - C‑level – see all data.
  - VP Sales – see data for their regions.
  - AE – see only their own deals and accounts.
  - CSM – see only their own customers.
- **Publishing:** Dashboards published to Tableau Server, accessible via corporate portal.
  Embedded in the control panel (iframe) for operators.

**5.4.4.2. Salesforce Dashboards**

- Standard reports and dashboards used for quick access within CRM.
- Reports built on custom fields created in Section 5.1.
- Automatic distribution: subscribed users receive weekly digests (PDF) by email,
  generated by reporting agents (Level 4).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
**CONCLUSIONS ON REPORTS AND DASHBOARDS**

- Dashboards cover all management levels: strategic (Executive), operational (Sales),
  and customer (CS).
- Tableau with live connection ensures data is current in real time.
- Built‑in row‑level security ensures each user sees only the data they are permitted to see.
- Automated report preparation (via reporting agents) reduces manual RevOps effort
  and accelerates decision‑making.
