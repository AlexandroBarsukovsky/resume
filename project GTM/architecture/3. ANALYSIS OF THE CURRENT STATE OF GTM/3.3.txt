**3.3. PROCESSES: FROM LEAD TO DEAL, FROM ONBOARDING TO EXPANSION**

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3.1. PROCESS FROM INTENT SIGNAL TO QUALIFICATION (SALES STRATEGIST)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **Collection of intent signals** – 6sense records activity: solution searches,
   product page visits, competitor queries.
2. **Export to Excel** – Strategist manually exports data, filters by ICP
   (Clearbit/ZoomInfo).
3. **Enrichment** – checks product activity (query to Snowflake).
4. **Prioritization** – manual assessment based on intuition and simple rules.
5. **Outreach** – Strategist creates a sequence in SalesLoft,
   sends personalized messages.
6. **Qualification** – if response is positive, Strategist creates
   an Opportunity in Salesforce and hands it over to the AE.

**Time estimates:**
- Collection and enrichment: 30–60 min per 100 accounts.
- Signal reaction time: up to 2 days.
- Intent → Opportunity conversion: ~15–20%.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3.2. PROCESS FROM QUALIFICATION TO DEAL (AE)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **Opportunity receipt** – AE receives a notification (email, task in Salesforce).
2. **Demo** – a meeting with the potential client is scheduled and held.
3. **MEDDPICC qualification** – AE fills in Opportunity fields (metrics,
   economic buyer, decision criteria, decision process,
   paper process, pains, champion, competition).
4. **Negotiation** – terms, prices, deadlines are discussed.
5. **Contract** – lawyers prepare the agreement (Ironclad), send for signing.
6. **Closing** – when signed, the Opportunity is moved to Closed Won,
   a task for the CSM is created.

**Time estimates:**
- Deal cycle: 90–120 days.
- Win rate: ~20% (enterprise).
- Forecast accuracy: 70–80% (subjective).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3.3. PROCESS FROM CLOSED DEAL TO ONBOARDING (CSM)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **Automatic notification** – when the status is Closed Won in Salesforce,
   a task for the CSM is created (via a process, but without automatic transfer
   of all data).
2. **Contact with the client** – CSM reaches out within 2–3 days, conducts
   an introductory meeting.
3. **Onboarding** – training, assistance with integration setup, setting
   target metrics (time to first value).
4. **Regular check‑ins** – CSM meets with the client every 1–2 weeks
   to track progress.

**Issues:**
- No automatic transfer of product data (usage, activity).
- Health score is not calculated; risks are identified subjectively.
- Time to first value averages 30–45 days (higher than target).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3.4. EXPANSION PROCESS (AM)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **Identifying opportunities** – AM manually reviews reports on client
   activity, requests for new features, growth in user numbers.
2. **Proposal preparation** – a commercial proposal is drafted.
3. **Negotiation** – expansion terms are discussed.
4. **Closing** – a new Opportunity or an amendment to the contract is created.

**Issues:**
- No automatic expansion signals.
- Missed opportunities due to manual monitoring.
- Expansion ARR below potential.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.3.5. CURRENT CUSTOMER LIFECYCLE SCHEMA
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Intent] → [Sales Strategist] → [AE] → [CSM] → [AM]
          (manual)   (MEDDPICC)   (manual)   (manual)

**Key gaps:**
- No automatic data transfer between stages.
- Lack of a unified system for end‑to‑end analytics.
- Manual handoffs create delays and information loss.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
**PROJECT CONCLUSIONS**
- All processes suffer from manual work and lack of automation.
- The target architecture must provide event‑driven stage transitions,
  automatic qualification, health score, and expansion signals.
- Reducing reaction time and deal cycle is only possible by eliminating
  manual operations.
