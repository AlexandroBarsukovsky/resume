**6.3. EXPANSION SIGNALS (SIGNALS FOR UPSELL/CROSS‑SELL)**

Expansion Signals are automatically detected indicators of a customer’s readiness to
expand an existing contract (upsell – increasing volume, cross‑sell – purchasing
additional products, such as Deep Search or MCP). These signals enable the Account
Manager (AM) to proactively offer new opportunities, increasing net retention and
reducing the risk of losing the customer to competitors.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.3.1. TRIGGER CONDITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Expansion Signal is activated (the flag `Expansion_Signal__c = true`) when **at least
one** of the following conditions is met.

**Condition 1 – High activity and user growth**
- **Activity:** `U > 0.7` (product usage above average).
- **User growth:** `(new_users_last_30d / users_previous_30d) – 1 > 0.3`.
- **Interpretation:** The customer actively uses the product and is growing their team,
  creating natural demand for additional licenses or features.

**Condition 2 – Request for additional functionality**
- The customer explicitly expresses interest in a new feature or product. Sources:
  * Demo request (`Demo_Request__c` in Salesforce, created via web form or by CSM).
  * Feature request (`Feature_Request__c` on Account, filled by engineering team or CSM).
  * Mention in a support ticket (Zendesk) of keywords: “upsell”, “additional license”,
    “Deep Search”, “MCP”, “increase limit”.
- **Interpretation:** The customer initiates a discussion about expansion, giving the
  highest readiness signal.

**Condition 3 – Contract renewal with increased volume**
- An Opportunity of type `Renewal` is created, and its amount > 110% of the previous
  contract amount (stored in `Previous_Contract_Amount__c`).
- **Interpretation:** The customer themselves proposes a budget increase at renewal.

**Condition 4 – Use of pilot features**
- The customer activates features that typically lead to expansion (e.g.,
  `Deep_Search_Active__c = true` and usage > 50% of the free tier limit). This condition
  is defined by product‑specific rules.

**Additional (configurable) conditions**
- RevOps operators can add additional rules via the control panel, for example:
  participation in an early‑adopter program, positive NPS > 9, request to increase the
  number of repositories, etc.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.3.2. AUTOMATIC ACTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

When the `Expansion_Signal__c` flag is set to `true`, the following automatic actions are
executed. All are asynchronous and managed by agents (Level 4).

**1. Create a task for the Account Manager**
- **Type:** Task (Salesforce object)
- **Subject:** `Expansion opportunity for [Account Name]`
- **Priority:** High
- **Owner:** Account Manager assigned to the account (field `AM_Owner__c`)
- **Description:** Specifies the trigger reason (which condition was met) and recommended
  actions (e.g., “offer Deep Search”, “conduct a demo”).
- **Due Date:** within 2 business days.

**2. Prepare a commercial proposal (financial agents)**
- Financial agents (Level 4) calculate the potential expansion volume based on:
  * Current consumption (if metrics are available: number of users, queries, repositories).
  * Historical data on expansion for similar customers (logistic regression).
  * Standard prices and possible discounts (depending on the ideology – conservative or aggressive).
- A JSON proposal template is generated and stored in Salesforce fields
  `Proposed_Expansion_Amount__c` and `Proposed_Products__c`.

**3. Update dashboards and notifications**
- An “Expansion Opportunities” widget appears in the AM dashboard, showing customer cards.
- A notification is sent to the Slack channel `#expansion-opportunities` with a brief
  summary: customer, amount, reason.
- If the AM does not close the task within 5 business days, an escalation task is created
  for the manager (VP of CS) and a reminder notification is sent.

**4. Launch a Gainsight playbook (optional)**
- Gainsight may launch the “Expansion” playbook, which sends a personalized message to
  the customer, containing links to additional materials and an offer for a consultation.

**5. Logging and analytics**
- All actions (task creation, proposal, notifications) are logged in Snowflake.
- Analytic agents collect statistics on signal activation and conversion to closed deals,
  which are used to retrain the model (adjust condition weights).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.3.3. ALGORITHMIC IMPLEMENTATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**Monitoring and signal computation**
- **Analytic agents (Level 1)** recalculate conditions 1, 2, and 4 weekly (on Sundays)
  for all accounts with `Health_Score__c > 0.7` or with expansion history. Results are
  stored in Snowflake.
- **Event‑based check for condition 2:** when a `Demo_Request__c` or `Feature_Request__c`
  is created, a Platform Event is published, which immediately triggers an agent to
  update the flag.
- **Condition 3** is checked when an Opportunity of type `Renewal` is created, via a
  Salesforce trigger (Apex) that publishes an event.

**Flag management**
- The `Expansion_Signal__c` flag is updated via Hightouch (reverse ETL) when the
  corresponding data changes in Snowflake.
- If a signal was active but did not lead to a deal within 30 days, the flag is
  automatically cleared (provided the conditions are no longer met).

**Threshold configuration via the control panel**
- RevOps operators can change the threshold for condition 1 (`U` and user growth),
  add/remove keywords for condition 2, and adjust the coefficient for condition 3.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
**CONCLUSIONS ON EXPANSION SIGNALS**

- Expansion Signals enable proactive identification of revenue growth opportunities
  from existing customers.
- The four types of conditions cover both behavioral signals (activity, growth) and
  explicit signals (requests, renewals).
- Automatic actions (tasks, proposals, notifications) reduce AM response time from
  weeks to hours.
- Flexible configuration via the control panel allows signals to be adapted to
  changing business priorities.
